About

I am a Ph.D. student working on computer networks, with a focus on computing-aware networking, network-layer computation-oriented communication, programmable data planes, and edge intelligence. My research explores how networks can better coordinate communication and computational resources for emerging distributed applications.

Research Interests

  • Computing-aware Networking and Service-oriented Routing
  • Network-layer Computation-oriented Communication
  • P4-based Programmable Data Plane
  • Anycast, Stateful Communication, and Scalable Routing
  • Edge Intelligence and AI-assisted Network Optimization

Selected Publications

  • P4Net: Facilitating Computation-oriented Communication Using P4-based SDN. Under review / Manuscript.
  • Srvcast: A Stateful Anycast Solution for Integrating Communication and Computation. Under revision / Manuscript.

Projects

  • P4-based Stateful Service Forwarding

  • Edge Intelligence for Distribution Network Applications
