About

I am a Ph.D. student at National Engineering Research Center of Advanced Network Technologies, Beijing Jiaotong University. My research focuses on computer networks, especially computing-aware networking, network-layer computation-oriented communication, programmable data planes, and edge intelligence.

I am interested in designing network mechanisms that coordinate communication and computational resources for emerging distributed applications.

Research Interests

  • Computing-aware Networking and Service-oriented Routing
  • Network-layer Computation-oriented Communication
  • P4-based Programmable Data Plane
  • Anycast, Stateful Communication, and Scalable Routing
  • Edge Intelligence and AI-assisted Network Optimization
